Our Story

Crafting Spaces,
Creating Stories

Dream Interior was born from a passion for transforming ordinary spaces into extraordinary living experiences. Based in Tezpur, Assam, we blend contemporary design with traditional craftsmanship to deliver interiors that are both beautiful and functional.

From concept to completion, every project is handled with meticulous attention to detail — ensuring your space reflects your personality and exceeds your expectations.

Every space has a soul. Our mission is to discover it, nurture it, and present it in its most beautiful form — crafted with love and precision.

Hiranya Ranjan Borah

Founder & Principal Designer — Dream Interior, Tezpur
